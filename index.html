About Founders

Meet Our Visionary Founder

Patilkaki was founded in 2020 by two college dropouts - Vinit Govinda Patil and Darshil Anil Savla, and Vinit's mother Geeta Govinda Patil. Their mission was to create a snack brand that reminds people of the taste of home.

Vision of founders

The founders' vision is to deliver their homemade snacks to pan India and take the concept of Patilkaki to every Indian home. The best-selling product is their modak, while Puran poli and other snacks are scalable.

What does PatilKaki do?

Online platform offering homemade snacks. It offers a variety of authentic Maharashtrian snacks, including protein ladoo, karanji/gujiya, and a regal sweet duo.

Location

How much funding has PatilKaki raised till date?
